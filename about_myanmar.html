About Myanmar

Welcome to Myanmar!!!

MINGALABAR!!!

Myanmar is a one of Mystical countries in Asia and new destination for tourist. Myanmar is situated between China and India. Myanmar has rich and long history, natural resources, also 90% of Myanmar people are Buddhist. There are so many Pagodas, temples, Monasteries, Nunnery and Ordination Halls. Myanmar has long costal line and beautiful beaches. Ngapali Beach is one of the famous beaches in Myanmar. It is situated in Rekhine State. There are 7 States and 7 Divisions. There are 62 millions population in 2012. There are 8 main national races; Kachin, Kayah, Kayin, Chin, Burma, Mon, Rakhine and Shan. Burma is a majority race. There are 65% in Myanmar; all are living in the whole country. There are more than 100 races in Myanmar. And there are more than 100 dialects. Myanmar is one of the agriculture countries; more than 70% are depending on agriculture. And also, there are forestry, mines and rivers.

There are 4 main rivers which are Ayeyarwaddy, Thanlwin, Chindwin and Sittoung. Ayeyarwaddy is a longest river in Myanmar, which has more than 2,000 km, Thanlwin is the second longest river, which has more than1,200 km, Chindwin is the third longest river , which has more than 800 km and the forth one is Sittoung river which has more than 600km. All are navigable.

Myanmar Ruby is very famous in the world. Mogoke and MaingShuu are very famous places because there are so many mines in there. And, Now Jade is one of the main export precious stone in Myanmar. There are 3 times in a year for Jade Emporium in Myanmar.

Natural Gas is one of the famous export things in Myanmar. Now, exporting to Thailand, China and India.

There are 3 seasons in Myanmar, which are hot season, rainy season and cool season. Hot season starts from March to end of May. Rainy season starts from Jun to end of September. Cool season starts from October to end of February. Cool season is the best for all tourists. Myanmar people are wearing longyi (sarong) always. Not only in day tiem but also in night time. Longyi is the best for all Myanmar people, because it is hot in the whole year round. Longyi is made of Cotton. So, Myanmar people are trying to keep their traditional costume.

Rice and curry are main food for all Myanmar people. Every day, Myanmar people eat Plain White Rice and Curry for lunch and dinner. It depends on the traditional of tribe, the taste is a little bit different. And the way of cooking style is a little bit different. Most of the tribes are talking their languages in their land. But, Burmese is a official language in Myanmar. And there are hill tribes in Northern Myanmar and Eastern Myanmar. Some hill tribes are animists. They are still celebrating their traditional festival in their places. When they celebrate their festival, they always wear their traditional costume.

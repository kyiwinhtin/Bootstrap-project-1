About Myanmar

Yangon

Yangon is a largest city in Myanmar. There are more than 6 millions who are living in Yangon. According to History, Yangon was built by King Alongpaya in 1755. At the time, called "Dagon" was a small fishermen village. And then, it was called "Rangoon "by British during British colonial period. It was changed again as like "Yangon" by Military Government in 1989. Means, "End of Strike". Yangon was a capital until 2006. "Shwedagon Pagoda, Sule Pagoda, Botahtaung Pagoda, Chaut Htat Gyi Reclining Buddha Image, National Museum, Gem Museum, Bo Gyoke Aung San Market, Kandawgyi Lake, Karawaik Royal Barge, Colonial Building Area, Yangon Port, Aung San's House Museum, Vegetable Market, Buddhist Nunnery and Buddhist meditation Center are tour sites in Yangon.

Around Yangon

Bago, Thanlyin, Twantay,Chaung Thar Beach and Ngwe Saung Beach are tour sites. Bago is 80km away from Yangon. Bago is a ancient city, which was built by King BayinNaung in 16 Century. It takes 1 and half hour by car. Thanlyin is 60km away form Yangon and it takes 1 hour by car. Thanlyin is a ancient city also, and during 16 century, there was a Portuguese mission in there. Twantay is 60km away from Yangon but it takes more than 1 hour. It is very famous for pottery village in Myanmar.

Bagan

Bagan is a one of the archeological sites in Asia. Bagan is the very important place for all tourist. Without seeing Bagan, no one can say about Myanmar. There were 55 Kings in Bagan History. But, there was a King in 11 Century, He was a King Anawyahtar. He was a founder of Myanmar First Dynastry in Bagan. And also, He was introduced Theravada Buddhism in Myanmar in 11 century. There are more than 2,000 temples and Pagodas in Bagan. All temples and Pagodas were constructed within 11 Century to 13 Century. According to Bagan History, there were more than 4,000 temples and Pagodas in Bagan Period. At that time, Bagan was Golden Period. The Bagan period was destroyed by Kubalai Khan in 13 Century. There was a big earthquake in 1975 in Bagan.

Around Bagan

Mt.Popa and Salay are tour sites around Bagan. From Bagan to Mt.Popa , it takes 1 hour by car and Bagan to Salay, it takes 2 hours by car. Mt. Popa was a extinct volcano in millions years ago. But, Mt.Popa is a very famous for Myanmar people who believe Spirit (local people called "Nat"). Mt.Popa is a one of the Nat House in Myanmar. There are festivals in Mt.Popa in August and April. There is a most beautiful teak monastery in Myanmar. It has more than 150 years and decorated with many wooden statues. On the way, enjoy the production of Petroleum in old style.

Mandalay

Mandalay is a second largest city in Myanmar. It is situated in the center of Myanmar. The old name was "Yadanarpon" means "full of precious stone". It was built in 1860 by King Mindom. Mandalay was a ex-capital because there was a Royal Palace. The last Royal Palace of Myanmar Kingdom and it was destroyed during the Second World War. It was occupied by British in 1885 during the Myanmar Last King Thibow. Mandalay is an Art of city, there are many handicrafts and Traditional Myanmar Silk Longyi is very famous. All tourists can enjoy Wood carving, Stone carving, Marionette, and Silk and Cotton weaving, Bronze casting, tapestry and Gold leaf making are very famous. Mahamuni Temple, Mandalay Royal Palace, Kuthodaw Pagoda, Golden Palace Monastery, Atumashi Monastery, KyautTawGyi Temple, Sandarmuni Pagada, Shwe Kyin Monastery, Shwe Inn Bin Monastery, Jade Market, Zaygyo Market and Night market are tour sites.

Around Mandalay

Amarapura

The second last capital in Myanmar. Amarapura means "Immortality". It was built in 18 Century. The first two foreign embassies were at Amarapura in 18 Century. There were British and French Embassies. It is situated southern part of Mandalay and Mahagandayon Monastery, U Bain teak wood Bridge and TaungTaman Lake are very famous for tourist.

Inwa (Ava)

One of the tour sites in Myanmar. After Bagan period, there was a Royal Palace in Inwa, it was built in 13 Century. It has more than 400 years long history. Horse cart riding is very significant and Yadanarseemee Pagoda, Bargayar Monastery, Inwa watch tower, Banana Plantation, and Meenu's Brick monastery are tour sites in Inwa.

Sagaing

There is a Sagaing Hill. There are more than 500 religious monuments such as Buddhist University, Monasteries, Nunneries and Meditation Center. It is situated west of Ayeyarwaddy River. There was a Royal Palace in 14 Century for a short time. Umin Thone See, Swon Oo Pone Nyashin Pagoda, Kaung Hmu daw Pagoda, Pottery and Silver-ware making are tourist sites.

Monywa

Monywa is a one of the tour sites in Myanmar. It is far from Mandalay, can reach by car. It takes 3 and half hours by car. Monywa is a Capital of Sagaing Division. It is situated east of Chindwin River. Thanbuddhe Temple, Boditahtaung, The world highest Standing Buddha Image, Huge Reclining Buddha Image, Phowin Taung, Shweba Taung are very famous for tourists.

Pyin Oo Lwin (Maymyo)

It is one of the beautiful cities in Myanmar. It is situated more than 1,000 m above sea level. The weather is pleasant and well-know "flowers city". It was built by Col May during the British Colonial. There is a Botanical Garden, the best place to observe several species of plants and flowers. MahaAtula Kan Htoo kan Thar Pagoda, Peaik Chin Myoung Cave, BE fall, Chinese temple and Myoma Market are very famous for visiting. And taking around with horse cart is very romantic. There are so many colonial houses, which are still in good conditions.

Hsi Baw

It is one of the tracking places for a foreigner who likes to visit to villages. The best way to reach to Hsi Baw is taking train. From Pyin Oo Lwin to His Baw by train which passes Goaik Htaik bridge. It is one of the amazing places in Myanmar. And then, continue to Hsi Baw by car. 2 Nights staying in His Baw is the best for tracking.

Mingun

River cursing from Mandalay to Mingun, is very significant and it take 1 hour by boat. Some time, can see Ayeyawwaddy Dolphin in Ayeyarwaddy River. Sometime, can see fishermen and Bamboo rafting in River. Mingun Temple, Mingun Bell and Sin Phyu Ma Shin Pagoda are tour sites.

Inle Lake

One of the amazing places in Myanmar. Inle Lake is second largest Lake in Myanmar. It is situated above 890m above sea level. There are more than 200 villages in Inle Lake and there are InnThar who lives in Inle Lake. Inle Lake has 22km long and 11 km wide and there are full of plantation. Most of them are floating. Tomatoes are one of the biggest productions from Inle Lake. Most of the houses are built in the Lake. They have canoes; they use it for plantation in floating gardens. All of Inn Thar people are rowing by leg when they go out by canoe. That's one is amazing and only one place in the world. There is very famous festival in October which is Phaung Daw Oo Pagoda festival. There is a 5 days market and so beautiful to see hill tribes with their traditional costumes. Pa Oh is one of the majority tribe in Shan State. They always wear Black color trouser and longyi with colorful turban.

Phaung Daw Oo Pagoda, Nga Phe Chaung Monastery, Silk weaving, Burmese Cheroot, Silverware, Lotus fiber weaving, Shwe Inn Daine, Sagar and Floating gardens are tour sites in Inle Lake.

Around Inle Lake

Kakku

Taung Gyi is a capital of Southern Shan state, there is a wine vinyl because the temperature is fine and fresh. There is a one of the famous tour site in Shan State, except Inle Lake, which name Kakku. It takes 3 hours from Inle Lake by car. Enjoy several plantations of vegetables. There are more than 2,000 Pagodas and temples. That is one of the new tour sites for foreigners. It was opened from 2006.

Kyaik-Hti-Yoo (Golden Rock)

It it situated 1,100m above sea level. It is very famous not only for local people but also for foreigners. It is known as Golden Rock Pagoda. That Pagoda is unbelievable because it is built on the cliff of mountain. It is a little bit far from Yangon. It takes 4 and half hours by car. From Base camp to second base camp, should take truck with local pilgrimages. And then, from second base camp to Pagoda Platform, it takes 1 hour on foot. Sun set at Kyaik Hti Yoo is very beautiful.

Around Kyaik-Hti-Yoo

There are Hpa An and Mawlamyaing are tours sites. There are many caves in Hpa An and enjoy the natural beauty on the way to Hpa An. Mawlamyaing is a third largest city in Myanmar. Kyaik Tha Lan Pagoda, Uzina Pagoda, Sein Done queen's monastery, the first Baptist Church, Myoma market are tour sites. There is a largest Reclining Buddha Image in Mudone. That is as like a Buddhist Museum because it can enter inside of the reclining Buddha Image. There is dead railway road near Mudone. That dead railway crossed on the river Quail which is border line with Thailand.

Mrauk U

One of the Archeological sites in Myanmar. It is situated in the Rakhine State. There are more than 2,000 Pagodas and temples. Some temples and Pagodas were constructed in 10 Century. Rakhine is one of the majority races in Myanmar. And all are Buddhists. There is a Royal Palace site excavation, Shit Thoung Pagoda, Koo Thoung Pagoda, Htout Kan Thein Pagoda are very famous. There are Chin Tattoo women. It is very interested to visit to their village by boat.

Around Mrauk U

Sittway is a capital of Rakhine State. Sittway is a main entrance of Mrauk U. There are many places to visit in Sittway. And also, Ngapali Beach is a one of the pleasant beaches in Myanmar.

Kyaing Tone

Kyaing Tone is a capital of Eastern Shan State. It can reach by flight from Heho and Mandalay. But, there is a one of the border town; name "Thachilaik". It takes 3 hours by car from Tarchilaik to Kyaing Tone. It is very famous for tacking. There are many hill tribes who are still living in mountain. Some are animists. There are Ah Khar, Ah Khu, Ann, Palaung, Shan, Shan Gyi, all are minorities' hill tribes. They are farmers and still wearing their traditional dress. Mahamuni Temple, Wet Inn monastery, Myoma market, Catholic Church, Thit Tabin gone, Naung Tone Lake, Hot spring and Shan style Lacquer ware workshop are very famous for tourist.

Beaches

There are 3 Beaches in Myanmar; these are Ngapali Beach, Ngwe Saung Beach, Chaung Thar Beach. Ngapali Beach is situated in the western of Myanmar. It takes 40 minutes by flight from Yangon. Ngwe Saung Beach is situated in Ayeyarwaddy Division. It takes 5 hours driving from Yangon.
